How it works

No paywall. No grind. Just sip and share.

1

Snap a boba photo

Open Boba Tea, frame your drink. The Boba-only mode auto-identifies what you're sipping.

2

Pull your card

Before you post, the pack opens. Drag and pinch to place the card on your photo. Double-tap to reset.

3

Collect them all

Cards land in your Ledger Pokedex with a parallax foil detail page. Tap "Owned" to see what you have, "All" to see what's still out there.
